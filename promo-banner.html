How to embed this in your site

WordPress (header.php): Paste the .promo-banner div right after the opening <body> tag, and add the CSS to your theme's stylesheet or Customizer → Additional CSS.

Elementor / page builder: Add an HTML widget at the very top of your page and paste the full banner markup + a <style> block.
